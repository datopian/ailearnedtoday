# TypeVibe (vibe.type)

Tiny design tool for exploring typography and "vibes" for landing pages.

![TypeVibe](https://screenshotit.app/https://typevibe.vercel.app)

## Links

- **Website**: https://typevibe.vercel.app

## Overview

TypeVibe (vibe.type) is a lightweight design playground for text styling and layout, focused on the *vibe* of landing pages. It fits alongside tools like [[aura-build]] and [[variant]] as part of the emerging **designapps** ecosystem: small, focused helpers for typography, layout, and design systems rather than full design suites.
