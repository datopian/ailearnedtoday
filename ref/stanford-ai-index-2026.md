Annual data-driven report on global AI progress, capabilities, economics, and policy.

## Overview

The **Stanford AI Index 2026** is the latest edition of the long-running AI Index series, which provides a comprehensive, data-driven snapshot of the state of AI worldwide. It tracks progress in:

- Technical capabilities and benchmarks
- Industry investment and economic impact
- Research output and talent flows
- Policy, regulation, and public perception

The 2026 report serves as a key reference point for policymakers, researchers, and industry leaders trying to understand how quickly AI is advancing and where the biggest shifts are occurring.
