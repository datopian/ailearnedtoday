## [[agent-psychosis]] - Armin Ronacher on AI Coding Addiction

Armin Ronacher's critical examination of "agent psychosis" — parasocial relationships with AI agents, dopamine-driven coding loops, open-source contribution quality degradation, and the brutal asymmetry of maintainer burden when reviewing AI-generated PRs.

## [[pew-ai-risks-regulation-2025]] - Expert vs. Public Views on AI Risks

Pew Research survey (April 2025) comparing AI experts and U.S. public on risks, opportunities, regulation — experts far more excited (47% vs. 11%), public increasingly concerned (51% vs. 15%), but both groups worry about insufficient regulation and lack confidence in government/industry to address AI responsibly.
