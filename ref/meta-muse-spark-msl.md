# Muse Spark (MSL)

Meta Superintelligence Labs' first model in the Muse series, powering a rebuilt Meta AI assistant with multimodal perception and parallel subagents.

## Overview

Meta announced **Muse Spark**, the first in a new series of models built by **Meta Superintelligence Labs (MSL)**, after rebuilding its AI stack "from the ground up" over the prior nine months.

Muse Spark is described as **small and fast by design**, but capable of reasoning through complex questions (including science, math, and health). It now powers the Meta AI assistant in the Meta AI app and meta.ai.

## What Changed in Meta AI

According to the announcement:

- **Modes**: Meta AI can switch between modes depending on task complexity
- **Parallel subagents**: Meta AI can launch **multiple subagents in parallel** to work on different parts of a question (e.g., planning a trip: itinerary + compare destinations + kid activities)
- **Multimodal perception**: Better ability to understand the world from photos (e.g., rank snacks by protein from an airport shelf)
- **Health**: Worked with physicians to improve helpfulness on common health questions (including some image/chart cases)
- **Visual coding**: Can generate custom websites and mini-games from prompts
- **Shopping + social context**: Pulls styling inspiration and context from content across Meta apps
